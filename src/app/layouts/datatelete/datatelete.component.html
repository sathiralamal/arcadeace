Data Policy

Effective Date: 2022/02/02

1. Introduction

Welcome to Arcadeace. This Data Policy outlines how we handle your personal data in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

2. Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data from our users. Your privacy is our priority, and we ensure that no personal information is gathered, stored, or shared.

3. User Rights

Under GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: You can request access to your personal data that we hold.
  • Right to Rectification: You can request correction of any inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Erasure: You can request deletion of your personal data.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: You can request restriction of processing your personal data.
  • Right to Data Portability: You can request transfer of your data to another service provider.
  • Right to Object: You can object to the processing of your personal data.

4. User Control

You have full control over your data. You can delete or remove any data associated with your use of our application at any time. We provide easy-to-use tools within the app to manage your data preferences.

5. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect any data you choose to share with us. Our security practices are designed to safeguard your information from unauthorized access and potential threats.

6. Data Retention

We do not retain any personal data. Any data you choose to share with us can be deleted at any time using the tools provided within the app.

7. Changes to This Data Policy

We may update this Data Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we will notify you of significant changes through our app or other communication channels.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Data Policy, please contact us at arcadeace@mail.com.
